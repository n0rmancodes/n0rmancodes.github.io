licensing

basics

all of my projects are open to the public domain, with or without credit. you do not need to contact me to ask to license it. this is because i learned from other people's work myself and i wouldn't want to restrict my code to the world. that and i hate the current state of copyright law. it feels immoral to lock off my work to the world.

hence, that's why all of current, past, and future work is (basically) in the public domain.
